About Us

Afolashade Aramide Akinsete

She started her career in the catering industry over 10 years ago, working in various restaurants and event spaces. She quickly gained a reputation for her attention to detail and her ability to create mouth-watering dishes that exceeded her clients' expectations.

Inspired by her success and her desire to bring her own personal touch to the catering industry, Afolashade decided to start her own business. With the support of her team, she has been able to turn her dream into a reality, offering catering services for events of all sizes.

In addition to our exceptional food, we also provide professional and reliable service. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that every event we cater is a success, from start to finish. We are thrilled to be able to bring our passion for catering to the Lagos community and look forward to making your next event a memorable one. Thank you for considering Dadecreamy Foodies And Events for your catering needs.
