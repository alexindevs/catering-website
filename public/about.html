About Us

Afolashade Akinsete

With over a decade of experience in top corporate organizations in Nigeria, she stepped out to set up a bespoke catering enterprise. She has undertaken various local and international training and successfully created a brand of excellent services for her client.

Her clientele base has grown rapidly using recommendations and guests who had a great feel of her services. She is well known for her attention to detail and her ability to create a variety of mouth-watering dishes which makes it almost impossible to have a favorite.

Our Mission

At Dadecreamy Foodies and Events, our mission is to provide the highest quality catering and event planning services globally.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading provider of catering and event planning services in Nigeria. We aim to be recognized for our exceptional food and event design, as well as our outstanding customer service. We believe in going above and beyond to ensure that every event is a success and leaves a lasting impression on our clients and their guests.

Our Team

Meet the Dadecreamy team! Our team is made up of enthusiastic caterers, and we are constantly striving to learn and grow in our roles. We are committed to delivering the best possible services to our clients and to constantly improving the way we do business.
