Fantastic Events

We take care of the hosting by providing tables & chairs, food, equipment like cutlery and silverware. Our staff will ensure everything is set up just the way you want it to be. Let Dadecreamy do all the work so you can enjoy!

Delicious Food

All our cooking is guided by a hungry stomach. We believe that what tastes good is healthy too (increasingly important in the Nigerian context) and that taste must be involved in every decision. We have dishes tailored to cuisine preferences.

Yummy Cakes

Fancy cake is delicious, but it's also can be difficult to present properly. Luckily, we're here to provide a range of delicious gourmet cakes that are presented in an elegant way. Your mouth will water when you see them!

About Us

Welcome to Dadecreamy, we are an event catering company that provides mouth-watering morsels of delightful food and desserts for all occasions. We've been in the game since 2006 and provide not just delicious food and desserts, but a friendly service and a venue that you can call your own. We provide catering, facilitation and venue rental services for events such as birthdays, weddings and corporate functions, with food options that include finger food, continental dishes, grilled meat, salad and more. We offer a wide range of desserts, including cakes, cupcakes, chocolates and more.
